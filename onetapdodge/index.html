One Tap Dodge – Support & FAQ

FAQ

How do I play?

Tap to swap lanes and dodge obstacles. Collect coins, complete challenges, and unlock new skins. Avoid obstacles to survive and increase your score.

What are coins used for?

Coins are earned during gameplay and can be spent to unlock new skins in the Customization screen. There are no in-app purchases — coins are only earned by playing.

How does the revive (watch ad to continue) work?

When you hit an obstacle, you can optionally watch a short video ad to revive and continue your current run. This is entirely optional. You can play again without watching.

My progress was lost. What happened?

Progress is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app or clearing app data will delete it. Make sure not to clear the app's storage if you want to keep your progress.

The app crashes or has a bug. What should I do?

Try restarting the app or your device. If the issue continues, contact us with your device model and a brief description of what happened. We'll do our best to help.

Is the app safe for children?

Yes. One Tap Dodge is designed for all ages. We use child-directed ad settings (no personalized ads, age-appropriate content only) and do not collect personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.
